Our Mission

The Main Street Bike Co-op fosters and maintains a healthy cycling community by educating cyclists to be self-sufficient & increasing cycling opportunities.

Health-wise we aim to get as many people using bikes correctly . . . so they can enhance their life through exercise. Economically, we help to provide bikes to people who would not normally be able to afford a bike.
